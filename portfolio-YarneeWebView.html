Yarnee

In 2014, i began working with Yarnee during development on their new social curation app. My first task was to design the First Time User Experience for the app, introducing the primary features of the app.

Further to this, i worked on UX improvements and delivered design proposals for the marketing product page and a web implementation of the Yarnee view.

Yarnee

The social curation app for iPhone.

For moments that belong together. Create as you do now, and then share your stories. Yanree loves your favourite apps as much as you do.
