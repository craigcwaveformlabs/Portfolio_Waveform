Scapa System

Scapa System is an interpretation and visualisation platform for Towed Streamer EM. At each stage of a project, Scapa System provides a set of tailored tools for completing specific tasks, from answering feasibility questions, through planning of an acquisition survey to the QC and visualisation of Towed Streamer EM data.

Date :2004-2014

Role :Project Lead

Client :PGS

Tools:Labview, Matlab, Sketch
