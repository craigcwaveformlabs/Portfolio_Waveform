About

Hi, I'm Craig Clarke. I am an Edinburgh based product designer and developer. I enjoy solving problems, designing clean interfaces and delivering working products.

I currently run Waveform, working on several design projects, such as Big Cheer. My previous work experience includes Yarnee (2014) and over ten years developing data visualisation and interpretation software with PGS (2004-2015)

Working with both multinationals and start ups, from concept to commercialisation, the lessons learnt have taught me how to move fast and iterate through experimentation. With my cross-disciplinary approach, I enable teams to craft meaningful products that solve real world problems.

Latest Projects

Scapa System

2004-2015

Towed Streamer EM. Resistivity in Context

As project leader, i had the responsibility for delivering a specialised data visualisation and interpretation platform supporting three generations of a breakthrough technology, from concept to commercialisation, from start-up to a multinational company.

I was involved across the full scope of the product, with responsibility for planning, mapping and leading toward a successful deployment. With a keen focus on innovative product design to overcome a unique challenge, i was able to deliver solutions that supported the growth and success of a new technology in a rapidly developing industry.

Big Cheer

2015

Celebrate Together with Big Cheer.

As Lead Product Designer, i enjoyed a central role in developing the design strategy and supporting development of the app from concept to delivery.

Working closely with the development team, I produced detailed design proposals to create an immersive and entertaining mobile experience tailored to the US sports market, with the inclusion of ESPNU mascots and college teams.

Yarnee

2014

For moments that belong together.

In 2014, i began working with Yarnee during development on their new social curation app. My first task was to design the First Time User Experience for the app, introducing the primary features of the app.

Further to this, i worked on UX improvements and delivered design proposals for the marketing product page and a web implementation of the Yarnee view.
